CHASE DELIVERABLE 3: LEAD SCORING (FORGE SCORE)
==================================================

Note: The full FORGE SCORE framework (0 to 10) was built in the Superagent session.
Below is the quick-reference version for daily use.

HOT (contact within 2 hours):
  Mentions a specific timeline ("need this done before [date]")
  Mentions a specific budget (any number stated)
  Referred by an existing FORGE client
  Has engaged with FORGE content multiple times before reaching out
  FORGE SCORE: 8 to 10

WARM (follow up within 24 hours):
  Completed contact form with business name and specific question
  Asked about a specific package (Standard or Full)
  Has a visible digital presence gap (checked their website: it is bad or nonexistent)
  FORGE SCORE: 5 to 7

COLD (standard CHASE cadence, no urgency):
  General enquiry: "just curious about what you do"
  No business name or website provided
  Vague timeline: "maybe later this year"
  FORGE SCORE: 1 to 4

DISQUALIFY (do not pursue):
  Business already has a strong digital presence (no gap to fill)
  Enquiry is from another agency or competitor
  Budget stated is below GBP 100 (incorrect expectation of the service)
  FORGE SCORE: 0
