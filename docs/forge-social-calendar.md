Caption:
Most small businesses lose 70% of their enquiries because they do not follow up correctly.

Not because the prospect went elsewhere. Because the prospect needed one more touchpoint that never came.

Here is what a working follow-up system looks like for a small business:

Day 0: Immediate email (auto) confirming the enquiry was received. Tell them what happens next.
Day 2: Follow-up email with one reason they are making the right choice.
Day 3: Phone call. Not a pitch. Just: "Did the email make sense?"
Day 5: DM or LinkedIn message. Personal, short.
Day 7: Email with a useful tip or insight. No hard sell.
Day 14: Final email. Honest and direct. One door left open.

That is 6 touchpoints. That is what converts a "thinking about it" into a signed client.

FORGE builds this system in full for every business we deploy. Written scripts for every touchpoint. Configured in HubSpot. Ready to run from day one.
