### Post 11 — Monday 7 July | LinkedIn | EDUCATION
**Content type:** Text post
**Topic:** What a good follow-up system looks like

**Caption:**
Most small businesses lose 70% of their enquiries because they do not follow up correctly.

Here's what actually happens:

Day 0: Customer submits a contact form.
Day 0: You get a notification, plan to reply later.
Day 2: You're on a job. You forget.
Day 5: Customer has already hired someone else.

This isn't a sales problem. It's a system problem.

A proper follow-up system looks like this:

→ Email 1 (Day 0, automatic): "Thanks for reaching out. Here's what happens next."
→ Email 2 (Day 2, automatic): "Here's why we're the right choice for your job."
→ Call attempt (Day 3): 60-second warm call using a short script.
→ Email 3 (Day 4, automatic): A genuine customer story.
→ Email 4 (Day 7, automatic): A useful tip that builds trust.
→ Email 5 (Day 10, automatic): Soft CTA — "Still thinking? Here's the easiest next step."
→ Email 6 (Day 14, automatic): Final, honest, no-pressure last message.

Every enquiry that comes through a FORGE-built website gets this automatically.

That's the difference between a website and a sales machine.
