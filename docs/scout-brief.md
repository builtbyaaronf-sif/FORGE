OPPORTUNITIES
  - 5.5 million SMBs in the UK: most have weak or no digital presence
  - Traditional agencies charge GBP 2,000 to 10,000 for what FORGE does in 20 minutes
  - Referral flywheel: every client becomes a case study and referral source

  - Content about "AI agency" is underserved in the trades and local business niche
