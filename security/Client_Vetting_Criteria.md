# FORGE — Client Vetting Criteria
**Run at the sales stage, before any work begins.**

The goal is to identify clients whose businesses could create legal exposure, reputational damage, or security risk for FORGE.

---

## Step 1 — Business Legitimacy Check

Run these checks before the discovery call:

| Check | How |
|---|---|
| Business exists and is trading | Companies House (UK): companieshouse.gov.uk |
| Social media presence is genuine | Check LinkedIn, Instagram — does the brand look real? |
| Website (if existing) isn't flagged | Run through Google Safe Browsing: transparencyreport.google.com |
| No news of fraud, scams, or legal action | Google: "[Business Name] scam / fraud / court" |

**If any check raises concern:** ask the client directly before proceeding. A legitimate business won't mind the question.

---

## Step 2 — Sector Red Flags (Decline or Escalate)

**Automatic decline — do not take on:**

- Adult content (explicit material, escort/dating with sexual content)
- Unlicensed financial services (crypto investment schemes, unlicensed lending)
- Weapons, ammunition, or illegal drug sales
- Counterfeit goods or IP infringement
- Pyramid schemes or MLM with no physical product
- Gambling (unless fully licensed by the UK Gambling Commission — verify)
- Political campaign sites

**Escalate to review (Aaron decides case-by-case):**

- Regulated industries: legal services, medical advice, financial advice — these require disclaimers and likely specific compliance language
- Nutraceuticals / supplements with medical claims
- Alcohol e-commerce (age gating required)
- Debt collection or credit services

---

## Step 3 — Data Collection Risk Assessment

Ask the client:

1. Will your site collect personal data? (names, emails, phone numbers) → **Standard — DPA required**
2. Will you collect sensitive data? (health, financial, children's data) → **High risk — legal review required before FORGE proceeds**
3. Will you process payments on the site? → **FORGE does not build payment processing into hosted sites. Client must use Stripe/PayPal hosted checkout. No card data can pass through FORGE infrastructure.**
4. Will you run targeted advertising? → **Google/Meta pixel tracking requires cookie consent — ensure banner is configured**

---

## Step 4 — Sales Stage Questions

Ask these conversationally during the discovery call. Flag answers that don't add up.

| Question | What you're looking for |
|---|---|
| "What does your business do, day to day?" | Clear, plausible business model |
| "Who are your typical customers?" | Real identifiable audience |
| "Do you have any existing branding or website?" | Consistency with what they've told you |
| "Have you worked with agencies before?" | If yes: why did they leave? Red flags in their answer? |
| "Where are you based and registered?" | Should match Companies House |

## Handling a Decline

If a client is declined:

- Be brief and professional: *"After reviewing your requirements, we don't think we're the right fit at this time."*
- Do not give detailed reasons (avoids disputes).
- Do not refund any paid discovery fee unless there is a clear contractual obligation.
- Log the decline and reason in the Client Record for future reference.
